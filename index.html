About Me

I am a builder. I have put together everything from computers to homes to menus. These days I focus on web apps. I'm stocking my toolbox with Python/Flask and Node.js/Express for the server allong with React and jQuery for the browser. My markup is semantic and my stylesheets are Sassy! Let's build something beautiful together.

When I'm not pounding the keys, you'll likely find me outside. My favorite sports are bicycling and whitewater kayaking, though I've deveoped a bit of a running habit. If I want to take it slow, I'll just grab my camera and get lost somewhere.
